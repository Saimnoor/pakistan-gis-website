About Pakistan Study Spaces GIS

This GIS-powered platform helps Pakistani students find optimal study environments across all provinces. Our mission is to improve educational accessibility by mapping all available study resources.

Pakistan Coverage:

Punjab Sindh Khyber Pakhtunkhwa Balochistan Islamabad Gilgit-Baltistan Azad Kashmir

Features:

  • Comprehensive database of study spaces across Pakistan
  • Real-time availability and crowd information
  • Advanced GIS tools for spatial analysis
  • Mobile-friendly PWA interface
  • Community reviews and ratings system
  • Booking system for study spaces
